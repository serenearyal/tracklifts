The log

Log every set in seconds.

Open a session, drop in exercises, and tap out your reps and weight. TrackLifts tallies your lifts, sets, and total volume automatically — so the math never slows you down between sets.

  • Auto totals for lifts, sets & volume — every session.
  • One-tap Repeat Workout to run yesterday again.
  • A history where the date is the hero — big "JUN 8", weekday above.

The library

One library. Every movement & every split.

A single tab, a clean segmented switch. Flip to Exercises for 69 built-ins across 7 muscle groups — search, favorite with a gold star, add your own, spot bodyweight moves by their BW badge. Flip to Splits to build routines like Push / Pull / Legs.

  • Search, favorites & fully custom exercises.
  • Build splits with days, reorder lifts, bulk-favorite.
  • Color-coded by muscle group, BW badges for bodyweight.

The numbers

Watch your numbers climb.

Swift Charts plot your max weight, estimated 1RM, and volume over time. Slice it by range — All, 90 days, or 30 days — for a windowed % change, then scope to everything you track, your favorites, or a single split.

  • Three metrics: max weight, est. 1RM & volume.
  • Time ranges with a windowed % change.
  • Scope by Tracked, Favorites, or per-Split.
