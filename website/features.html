LOGGING THAT
KEEPS UP WITH YOU

A workout is a session of exercises, and each exercise is a stack of sets — just reps and weight. Add them as fast as you can hit them. The math runs itself.

  • Sets that take two tapsPunch in reps and weight per set; reorder exercises with a drag when your plan changes mid-session.
  • Totals, automaticallyEvery session sums its lifts, sets, and total volume the moment you log — no spreadsheet, no mental math.
  • Repeat WorkoutBring back a previous session in one tap, then beat it. Yesterday becomes the floor, not the ceiling.

Questions

THE STRAIGHT ANSWERS

Is TrackLifts free?+

Yes — download it from the App Store and start logging. No paywall stands between you and tracking your lifts.

Do I need an account, or does it sync?+

No account, no sign-up, no cloud login. TrackLifts is built to be opened and used. Your training history lives in the app on your iPhone — there's nothing to log into.

Does it work offline?+

Completely. Every feature — logging, the library, charts, PRs — runs locally with zero network required. The basement gym with no signal is no problem.

Can I track bodyweight exercises?+

Yes. Mark a movement as bodyweight and it carries a clear BW badge in the library. You can also add your own custom exercises whenever the catalog is missing one.

Kilograms or pounds?+

Both. Choose kg or lb in Settings and every weight, total, and chart follows your preference throughout the app.

Is there an Apple Watch app?+

Today TrackLifts is a focused iPhone experience, designed to give every metric room to breathe on a full screen. A wrist companion is on the radar — for now, your phone is home base.
